# Write a mission chain about gathering alien technology to construct the Ultra-Sick Turbo Flivver
mission "Ultra-Sick Turbo Flivver 1"
	shipyard
	source "Makerplace"
	invisible
	to offer
		has "flagship attribute: 'jump drive'"
	'Uncle Trusty slides over to you in the shipyard. "Hey there, captain. You're looking well-travelled - are my ears failing me, or did I detect the telltale hum of a..." He pauses, holding up a defensive paw and making an exaggerated show of looking around for eavesdroppers. With a nod, he puts that paw on your back and guides you away from the other people doing their shipyard business. "...A Jump Drive? Yes, yessss I did." This slick Hai now rubs his paws together in anticipation of an exciting deal. "You are clearly a captain of fine taste, you wouldn't be here otherwise! Now, I appreciate human ships. Your people have many designs sleek and fearsome. You're acquainted with my Rav, of course. The trimmed Raven? It's something of a hobby of mine, taking such alien ships and...upgrading them." Another pause from Uncle Trusty. He walks a little circle, visibly gathering his thoughts and reining in the natural Hai tendency to speak at great speed to a more human pace. "I've had my eye on the Flivver. Beautiful little ship. Absolutely beautiful. I want to do this one right. Not just Hai technology, no. This one I want to take all the way. Make it the best little beauty it can possibly be." Clap! Sounds a bit funny coming from fuzzy little paws, but it still draws a sharp line in the conversation. "Now! This is what I'm asking: you travel the galaxy with your jump drive, bring me the tech I ask for - at your own expense! - and when we are done I'll let you fly it. A ship like this, I know I'm not good enough to make her sing, but I'm betting you are. Deal?"'
	'Uncle Trusty extends a paw. Shake on it?'
